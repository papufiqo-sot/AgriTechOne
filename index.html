Call for Submissions

Abstracts, Videos
and Posters

AgrTech-One welcomes submissions from researchers, PhD students, startups & practitioners worldwide. Send Presentations to scientiae-naturalis-registration@yahoo.com to participate. The call prioritises the Global South: 12 full scholarships covering the APC are available for participants from Low-Income Countries and the Global South. Every accepted submission receives a citable DOI.

Every accepted poster is assigned a unique DOI. All presentations are evaluated by three independent experts. Proceedings carry both ISBN and ISSN, indexed on Google Scholar. A volunteer team of 120 researchers provides subtitles and technical support. Authors may link their ORCID identifier. Institutions are verified via ROR. Each participant receives a blockchain-verified Digital Certificate of Participation. The conference follows EU Metadata Policies and Standards throughout. The Scientific Advisory Committee (SAC), that serves as a high-level body of distinguished experts, will provide the strategic guidance to ensure the event's academic integrity and quality

Institutional Partners

Conference Organizers

The AgriTech-One Global Conference 2026' is organized by the Scientiae Naturalis partner consortium.

AgriTech-One is a structured around the Strategic Dialogue on the Future of EU Agriculture initiative. The conference has been awarded ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, and IQNET certifications.

The conference is ORCID Compliant and ROR Integrated. Authors are invited to include their ORCID identifier at submission. Institutional affiliations are verified via the Research Organization Registry (ROR).

The organising infrastructure rests on a distributed volunteer model. A team of 120 researchers from universities and startups across the Global South handles peer review, editorial support and subtitle production.

12 scholarships are reserved for participants from Low-Income Countries and the Global South.
